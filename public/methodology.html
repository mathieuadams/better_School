Our Methodology

Understanding how we calculate fair, transparent, and comprehensive school ratings

Rating Overview

FindSchool.uk uses a comprehensive 1-10 rating system that combines multiple data points to provide a fair assessment of each school. Our methodology is designed to be transparent, contextual, and reflective of the multiple dimensions that contribute to school quality.

Rating Scale Interpretation

9-10
Outstanding
Exceptional performance across all measured areas. These schools consistently exceed expectations.
7-8
Good
Above average performance with strong outcomes. Most areas meet or exceed standards.
5-6

Satisfactory performance meeting expected standards. Mixed strengths and areas for improvement.
3-4
Below Average
Performance below expected standards. Significant areas requiring improvement.
1-2
Poor
Substantial underperformance. Urgent improvement needed across multiple areas.

Calculation Process

Step 1: Data Normalization

All metrics are normalized to a 0-10 scale to ensure fair comparison:

  • Academic scores: Percentage scores are converted to 0-10 scale
  • Inspection ratings: Outstanding=10, Good=7, Requires Improvement=5, Inadequate=3
  • Attendance: Scaled relative to local authority average (95%+ = 10)

Step 2: Weighted Average

Special Considerations

Scotland

Scottish schools use a modified methodology due to different assessment systems:

  • Academic performance weighted at 60%
  • Attendance weighted at 40%
  • Education Scotland inspection ratings when available

Special Schools

Special Educational Needs schools are evaluated with adjusted metrics that account for their unique context and student population. Progress measures are weighted more heavily than attainment.

New Schools

Schools less than 3 years old may have limited data. We clearly mark these schools and base ratings only on available information, updating as more data becomes available.

Transparency Commitments

We believe in complete transparency about our ratings:

  • Data sources: All data sources are clearly identified on each school profile
  • Component breakdown: Users can see how each factor contributes to the overall rating
  • Data freshness: Last update dates are displayed for all metrics
  • Completeness indicators: We show when ratings are based on partial data
  • No hidden factors: Our entire methodology is public and open to scrutiny

Limitations & Disclaimers

While our ratings provide valuable insights, they have limitations:

  • Ratings cannot capture all aspects of school quality (e.g., culture, extracurriculars)
  • Data may be up to 12 months old depending on government release cycles
  • Context matters - a lower-rated school might still be the best fit for your child
  • Private schools may have limited publicly available data
  • Ratings should be one of many factors in school selection

Important: We strongly recommend visiting schools, speaking with staff, and considering your child's individual needs when making school choices. Our ratings are a starting point for research, not a definitive judgment.
